### Fixed
- **Bug 1 â€” currency no longer caches a failed registry lookup as a fresh fact** (bughunt 2026-06-13): an errored npm/PyPI lookup (network/HTTP/timeout, all collapsed to `undefined`) was unconditionally stamped `fetchedAt: today()`, so `isFresh()` reused it for the full 7-day TTL (never retried) and `audit-docs` read a `latest: undefined` entry as a present, current fact â€” a fail-OPEN violation of the "inconclusive = FAIL" contract.
